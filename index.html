About Me

After leaving school I went to study engineering at university, but I didn't enjoy it and wanted to find something that was more creatively minded. I chose to do animation over other art principles as I felt that I could use some of the skills that I had learned from my engineering in my animations. I have overcome many challenges that I faced in creating my works, such as learning new programmes in short periods of time and finding new and creative way to make it when time simply won't allow for the animation I had planned. I am currently studying Digital Media Production at Robert Gordon University in Aberdeen.This course will enable to me to continue to build on the skills that I leanred at college, as well as adding new skills such as programming for websites and illustration.



Neal Robertson Animations 2017
